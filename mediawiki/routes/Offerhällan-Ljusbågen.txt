==In English==
(''Electric arc'') The route starts on a ledge which is approached by rapping in from a bolted belay just south of the lean-to shelter. Use two 60 m ropes to rap in as 50 m ropes may not suffice. Two pitches (6-,6+). A standard trad rack will suffice.
