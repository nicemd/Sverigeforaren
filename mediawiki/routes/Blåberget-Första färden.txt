'''Rack''': Något jättestort, typ Camalot #4 och/eller #5 är skönt i starten (vilket i och för sig är helt uppenbart...).

==In English==
(''Premier journey'') The first route on Blåberget climbed in the early 80's as a winter aid route. Climb the steep inside corner through an overhang and up a chimney to a bolted belay at the top. Move west along the ledge to a better rap-station, or climb to the top via the second pitch of ''Sista färden''.  Bring something really big for the first (easy) part of the route, like a Camalot #4 and/or #5.
