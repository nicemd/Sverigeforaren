==In English==
A masterpiece! Vertical face-climbing in 80's style.  Protected by an assortment of small wires, TCUs and bolts.  Always slightly runout, but not dangerously so; very technical with a smattering of cruxes.  A clean ascent is harder to come by than the grade would lead you to believe. 35 m.  Walk off or rap of tree at the top. A 70 m rope will take you down, just.  One of the best routes in [[Västerbotten]].
