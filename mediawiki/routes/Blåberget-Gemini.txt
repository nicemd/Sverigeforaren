'''Rack''': Ett set kil, fem friends och 12 kortslingor duger gott

==In English==
(''Gemini'') Start as ''Fallicruxus'' but traverse left along the horizontal crack that splits the entire wall. Take a belay at a big somewhat loose rock by some bolts. The second pitch has some bolts and pins.
'''Gear''': A set of wires and five friends.
