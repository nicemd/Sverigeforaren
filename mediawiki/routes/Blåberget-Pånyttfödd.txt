Den markanta, mycket breda taksprickan som leder upp till Kojhyllan. Klättrare med byxlinning över 30 tum göre sig knappast besvär, icke heller de som saknar intresse för lidande. Camalot #6, solo eller topprep.


===In English===
(''Born Again''). Climb the obvious, very wide roof-crack/chimney up to the ledge with the old hut.  Climbers with a waist size above 30 inches are advised to find something else to climb, as are those not interesting in the art of suffering. Camalot #6, solo or toprope.
