==In English==
An absolute classic.  The route starts from a ledge that is approached from the left.  '''P1''', 7. Start on the right side of the ledge and step up to the steep crack that is followed with increasing difficulties up towards an open book. Make a stance at a ledge at the bottom of the book. 20 m. '''P2''', 7-. Technical climbing in an open book.
