==In English==
Maybe the strongest line on Blåberget. The clean vertical crack that splits the entire ''Ikaros-face''. '''P1''', 6-, Climb the crack to a bolted belay on a ledge, a Friend #4 or Camalot #4 is good to have, 29 m.  '''P2''' Keep follow the crack, more face climbing on this pitch. 29 m.
