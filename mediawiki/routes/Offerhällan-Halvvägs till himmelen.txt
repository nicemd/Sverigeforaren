==In English==
(''Halfway to Heaven''). Climbs the obvious handcrack, one of the finest in Sweden, in the big flake to a bolted belay. Climb up right on the wafer-thin flexing hanging flake until it gets solid enough to place some protection. Continue up the corner to a bolted belay on a ledge, or keep going all the way to the top.
